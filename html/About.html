Eugénie

What's up Laylow visitors !

Let's look at my point of view about how talented and avant-garde Laylow is.

My name is Eugénie and I'm really into French music and especially French rap. From the most famous to the underground artists, I like to learn more about the ones that I don't know yet.

In my opinion, even though you are not really fan of melancholic music, you cannot deny the talent of such a rapper as Laylow. It's rare nowadays to see artists with a whole new universe to bring in a style that has already several decades.

To understand why I am so enthusiastic about Laylow, I invite you to check our webssite to learn more about this fabulous artist !
